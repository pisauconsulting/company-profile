COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked
Questions

We believe in full transparency. Here are answers to the questions we hear most from prospective clients.

How long does the Essential implementation take?+
Typically 1–2 months depending on the complexity of your operation, the quality of existing data, and the responsiveness of your team. We'll give a more precise timeline after our initial scoping conversation.
Do I need TSpoonLab before we start?+
No. Part of our scope includes setting up and configuring TSpoonLab for your operation — we handle technical onboarding and training so your team can use it effectively from day one. Note: the Advisory package does require an existing TSpoonLab setup.
Can I start with Essential and upgrade to Growth later?+
Absolutely. Many clients begin with Essential to establish their systems, then transition to Growth for ongoing monitoring. The move is seamless since all your data is already structured in our workflow.
What data do you need from us to get started?+
We need: POS Reports (Sales, Complimentary, Own Consumption), Recipe Costing Cards, Purchase Histories, Wastage and Loss Reports, an updated Supplier/Market List, and Inventory Valuation. Incomplete data is fine — identifying gaps is part of Phase 1.
How does the Gastronomy Launch project analysis work?+
Because this service demands 100% dedication from a single specialist, we evaluate each project individually before committing. We assess the scope, timeline, concept complexity, and team readiness. If we take it on, we are fully dedicated — if we cannot, we'll let you know honestly and may suggest an alternative path.
Is the Advisory package standalone or part of a larger engagement?+
Both. It's designed for venues that already have their systems in order (TSpoonLab included) and want expert ongoing monitoring. It also naturally follows the Essential or Growth implementation as a continuation.
Are your services available outside Bali?+
Yes — most of our services can be delivered fully online, making location no barrier and we're always open to discussing how we can best support your operation remotely or on-site. Reach out and we'll find the right approach for your situation.
What's your cancellation policy?+
All payments are strictly non-refundable. In the event of termination by the client, all payments up to that date are retained. Scope modifications must be agreed in writing by both parties and may result in fee adjustments.
How do you protect our business data?+
Both parties sign a strict confidentiality agreement at the start of every engagement. All operational, financial, and strategic data is treated with full discretion and will never be disclosed to third parties without your explicit written consent.
